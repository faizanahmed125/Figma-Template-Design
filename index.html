About

Award Winning Business Consultant | Public Speaker | Career Coach | Activist | Mentor

I'm Yvette Donado, Founder Of LifeSkills Lab.

The story of how this nonprofit came to be has roots reaching all the way back to my childhood. As the daughter of Puerto Rican parents who joined the wave of migration to the mainland in the 1950s, I was raised on the ideals of the American Dream—the very ideals that drew my parents to this country. My father used to say, “Do your best, work hard, and make sure you’re driven by your passions—not the pursuit of money.” I internalized that powerful message and believed that with enough effort, anyone could achieve anything.
